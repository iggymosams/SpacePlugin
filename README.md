##Crafting

**Advanced Redstone**
- 1 Comparator
- 1 Repeater
- 1 Redstone
- Shapeless

**Rocket Engine**
- 3 Iron Ingot (I)
- 1 Fire Charge (C)
  <br>
  ``#I#``
  <br>
  ``ICI``

**Space suit**
- Same as most armor but with advance redstone
- Space suit helmet needs oxygen mask in the middle

**Oxygen Mask**
- 1 Advanced Redstone
- 1 Glass
- 7 Iron, Diamond or Netherite (Depends on the tier)

**Rocket**
- 1 Rocket Engine (E)
- 6 Advance Redstone (R)
  <br>
  `#R#`
  <br>
  `RRR`
  <br>
  `RER`
